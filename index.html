About the Foundation

Knowledge in service of opportunity.

Organized and operated to conduct applied research — gaining the knowledge needed to meet a specific, recognized need.

Applied research includes investigations oriented toward discovering new scientific knowledge with specific commercial objectives in products, processes, or services. NEOFORTUNITY INC. directs that knowledge toward opening real opportunities for the people and communities it serves.

Our Purposes

Scientific, educational & charitable.

Organized and operated across three weighted commitments that guide every initiative we undertake.

Scientific50%

Offers supervision and training in the scientific education of college and university students, and publishes scientific information in treatises, theses, trade publications, and other forms available to the interested public.

IRS reference (Publication 5730)
Educational25%

Trains people in the specific skills they need to obtain employment in a particular industry, and provides work experience for youth and others who have no employment history.

IRS reference (Educational Organizations)
Charitable25%

Concerned with civil society, philanthropy, and the nonprofit sector — promoting charitable giving and voluntarism through advocacy, disclosure, information exchange, and research.

IRS reference (Publication 5781)
Research

Advancing knowledge in the public interest.

As a 501(c)(3) scientific organization, our findings are made publicly available on a nondiscriminatory basis.
